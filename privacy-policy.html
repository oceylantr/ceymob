Privacy Policy

Luxplate Icon Pack  ·  Last updated: March 17, 2026
Luxplate Icon Pack is built on the open-source CandyBar framework. We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

Permissions Used

The following Android permissions are requested by Luxplate. None are used to collect personal information.

Permission Purpose
INTERNET Downloading cloud wallpapers and checking for icon updates
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Checking network availability before loading remote content
ACCESS_WIFI_STATE Optimizing data usage on Wi-Fi connections
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Reading wallpapers stored on device (Android 12 and below)
SET_WALLPAPER Applying wallpapers selected by the user
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Running wallpaper download tasks in the background

Icon Requests

When you submit an icon request, the app generates an email pre-filled with basic device information (launcher name, Android version). This email is sent directly from your own email client to our support address. We do not intercept or automatically store this data.

Third-Party Services

Google Play Store handles all purchases and provides anonymized, aggregated analytics to us as a developer. No personally identifiable information is available to us through this channel.

GitHub may host cloud wallpapers. GitHub's own privacy policy applies to any content served from their platform.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
